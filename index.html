Research Interests

My long-term research interest is to connect mathematical analysis of numerical methods, AI systems that propose or assist with mathematical reasoning, and formal proof systems that can verify the resulting arguments.

I am especially interested in workflows where informal derivations, executable experiments, and formal certificates can be checked against each other rather than treated as separate artifacts.
