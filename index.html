Research

My recent work focuses on world models for embodied intelligence at Shenzhen Daohe Tongtai Robotics Co., Ltd. I am interested in large-model systems for agents that perceive, reason, plan, and act in physical environments.

Current research areas include vision-language-action models (VLA), LLM-based agents, WAM-style world/action modeling, multimodal perception, robotic planning, and embodied interaction. My earlier training is in computational mathematics, computational fluid dynamics, numerical PDEs, scientific computing, and AI for Science.
