Research

My current work focuses on large models for embodied intelligence, including LLM-based agents, vision-language-action models (VLA), WAM-style world/action modeling, and their connections to robotic perception, planning, and action.

My research background is in computational mathematics, computational fluid dynamics, numerical PDEs, scientific computing, and AI for Science. I am interested in how scientific-computing methods and modern foundation models can be combined for reliable modeling, simulation, decision-making, and embodied interaction.
